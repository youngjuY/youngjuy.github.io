Cello

I started playing the cello in 2019, and I joined the KAIST Orchestra in 2022. In fall 2024, while visiting in Austin, I performed with EChO, and I have occasionally appeared as a guest musician with the Daejeon Youth Orchestra. This hobby has been a wonderful part of my life.
