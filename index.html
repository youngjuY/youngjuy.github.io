Research

My research interest is in robot learning, building reliable robots that adapt to real-world tasks (household and office) from simple instructions such as language or demonstrations. My goal is to achieve generalization across unseen environments and tasks, approaching this from a machine learning perspective by leveraging foundation models to build adaptable robots.
