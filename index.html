Research

My research interest is in building reliable robots that adapt to real-world tasks (office and household manipulation and navigation) from simple instructions (e.g., language or demonstrations). My goal is generalization across unseen environments and tasks, including long-horizon tasks, with customization for human preferences.

I am approaching this problem from a machine learning perspective, specifically using foundation models.
